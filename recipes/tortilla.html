Tortilla

Easy homemade tortillas with tasty fillings

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 3/4 cups of water
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
For fillings
  • Chicken
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • leek
Making the tortillas
  1. Mix the flour and salt together
  2. Mix water and olive oil in to the flour
  3. Make a dough and add more flour till it's not sticky
  4. Let the dough rest under a blanket for 10min
  5. Cut the dough into 8 even pieces
  6. Make all the pieces round shaped with our hands
  7. Roll the pieces into medium sized tortillas
  8. Heat the tortillas in medium heat, untill brown,for 2min per side
Making the filling
  1. Cook the chicken and add spices of your taste
  2. Cut cabbage, carrot and leek into small put tall slices
  3. Mix the vegetables
  4. You can mix the vegetables with olive oil and vinegar if you like
