Carbonara

Easy homemade carbonara

  • Pancetta
  • Pecorino Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic cloves
Making the carbonara
  1. Boil water and add spaghetti for 8min when water is boiling
  2. Chop pancetta, grate pecorino
  3. Beat the eggs in one bowl
  4. Cook pancetta till brown, add garlic cloves
  5. When pancetta is ready add the boiled spaghetti and stir
  6. Add eggs and pecorino into pan and salt and pepper to your own taste
  7. Stir well and serve!
