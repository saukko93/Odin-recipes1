Meatballs

Easy homemade meatballs

  • Beef
  • Bread crumbs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
Making the meatballs
  1. Mix the raw beef with bread crumbs
  2. Add little bit of olive oil
  3. Add seasoning
  4. Heat oven to 200C
  5. Make medium sized meatballs with your hands
  6. Put the meatballs into an oiled pan
  7. Put the pan to oven for 20min
  8. Take the pan out and let rest for 10min
